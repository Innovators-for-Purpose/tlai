Think Like AI

Artificial Intelligence

The increasingly fast integration of AI into various parts of our lives- such as virtual assistants to autonomous cars- caused us to question the role AI has and it's ethicallity in society. The increasing number of cases where ethical issues within AI systems are brought to light, are a major cause for concern. This especially includes our main issue of concern, bias in AI.

Bias

Systemic bias in AI can affect the probability of a person being accepted in a school, getting a job, along with being arrested and many similar systems. We believe it is extremely important to recognize the power AI can have over our lives and how it's influenced by our own societial issues and bias. This is especially concerning due to how rapidly AI and technology entirely are progressing while it's ethics are lagging behind.
